UNESCO Vouraikos Gorge

The Railway Story

1896

Construction of the mountain railway begins, carving a technical wonder through the gorge.

1897

The first trains run between Diakopto and Kalavryta, opening a new era of travel and trade.

Today

The Odontotos remains one of Europe's most scenic rack-rail experiences for visitors worldwide.
